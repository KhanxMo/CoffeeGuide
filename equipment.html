Welcome!

Welcome to our Equipment page! Here, you'll find our recommendations for coffee and espresso machines, grinders, and accessories. Whether you're a coffee novice or a seasoned pro, we've got you covered.

Coffee Machines

  • Bonavita BV1900TS: This machine is a great choice for those who want a simple, no-frills coffee maker that produces consistently good coffee. It has a pre-infusion mode that wets the coffee grounds before brewing, which helps to extract more flavor.

Espresso Machines

  • Breville BES870XL Barista Express: This machine is a great all-in-one option for those who want to make espresso, cappuccinos, and lattes. It has a built-in conical burr grinder, so you can grind your own beans for the freshest coffee possible.
  • Rancilio Silvia: This machine is a popular choice among home baristas for its durability and ability to produce high-quality espresso. It has a commercial-grade group head and portafilter for maximum flavor extraction.
  • Gaggia Classic Pro: This machine is a great choice for those who want a quality espresso machine without breaking the bank. It has a commercial-style portafilter and advanced heating system for consistent temperature and great flavor.

Grinders

  • Baratza Encore: This grinder is a great all-around choice for those who want a quality grinder for both espresso and drip coffee. It has 40 individual grind settings, so you can adjust the grind to your specific needs.
  • Rancilio Rocky: This grinder is a great choice for those who are serious about their espresso. It has commercial-grade burrs and is built to last for years of heavy use.

Accessories

  • Milk Frothing Pitcher: A good milk frothing pitcher is essential for making cappuccinos and lattes. Look for one with a pointed spout for easy pouring.
  • Scale: A scale is a great tool for measuring your coffee and water to ensure the perfect ratio. Look for one with a built-in timer for even more precision.
  • Knock Box: A knock box is a convenient way to dispose of your used coffee grounds. Look for one with a removable and easy-to-clean container.

We hope this equipment guide has been helpful. With these recommendations, you're well on your way to brewing the perfect cup of coffee or espresso.
