Recipes

Welcome to our Coffee Recipes page! Here, you'll find a collection of coffee and espresso drink recipes, complete with step-by-step instructions. Whether you're in the mood for a classic latte or a trendy whipped coffee, we've got you covered.

Classic Latte

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1 cup of steamed milk
  • Sweetener of your choice (optional)
  1. Brew 1 shot of espresso and set aside.
  2. Steam 1 cup of milk until frothy.
  3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso.
  4. Add sweetener to taste (optional).
  5. Enjoy!

Cappuccino

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1/2 cup of steamed milk
  • 1/2 cup of milk foam
  1. Brew 1 shot of espresso and set aside.
  2. Steam 1/2 cup of milk until frothy and 1/2 cup of milk until it has a velvety texture.
  3. Pour the frothed milk over the espresso.
  4. Add the milk foam on top.

Iced Coffee

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • 1 cup of coffee, brewed and chilled
  • 1/4 cup of milk or cream
  • Sweetener of your choice (optional)
  • Ice
  1. Brew 1 cup of coffee and chill in the refrigerator.
  2. Fill a glass with ice.
  3. Pour the chilled coffee over the ice.
  4. Add milk or cream to taste.
  5. Add sweetener to taste (optional).

Whipped Coffee

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • 2 tablespoons of instant coffee
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of hot water
  • Milk
  • Ice
  1. In a bowl, mix together instant coffee, sugar, and hot water until smooth and frothy.
  2. Fill a glass with ice and milk.
  3. Spoon the whipped coffee mixture on top of the milk.
  4. Stir the coffee mixture into the milk before drinking.

We hope you enjoy these coffee and espresso drink recipes. Don't be afraid to get creative and experiment with different flavors and ingredients!
