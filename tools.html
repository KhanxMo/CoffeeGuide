Grind Size Guide

Grind Size Brew Method Description
Extra Coarse Cold Brew Very large, chunky coffee particles. Similar to peppercorns.
Coarse French Press Large coffee particles, with a texture similar to sea salt.
Medium-Coarse Clever Dripper Medium-sized particles, with a texture similar to coarse sand.
Medium Drip Coffee Medium-sized particles, with a texture similar to sand.
Medium-Fine Pour Over Small to medium-sized particles, with a texture similar to granulated sugar.
Fine Espresso Small particles, with a texture similar to powdered sugar.
Extra Fine Turkish Coffee Very small particles, with a texture similar to flour.

We hope this grind size guide has been helpful. Use this table to select the right grind size for your brewing method and take your coffee-making to the next level!
